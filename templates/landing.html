Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SmartReadAI

What file formats does SmartReadAI support?

Currently, SmartReadAI supports PDF and EPUB files. We're working on adding support for more formats including MOBI, TXT, and DOC in the future.

Is my book data secure?

Absolutely! We take data privacy seriously. Your uploaded books are encrypted and processed securely. We don't share or store the full text of your books beyond what's needed for analysis.

How accurate are the AI responses?

Our AI is trained on a vast library of literary works and provides highly accurate analyses. However, like any AI, it may occasionally misinterpret nuanced content. We're continuously improving our models for even better accuracy.

Can I use SmartReadAI for academic purposes?

Yes! Many students and educators use SmartReadAI to enhance their understanding of texts. It's a great study companion, but we recommend using it as a supplementary tool rather than a replacement for critical reading.

How many books can I upload?

There is no strict limit on the number of books you can upload and analyze. However, we recommend keeping your library to a manageable size for the best experience.
